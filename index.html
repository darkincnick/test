IGL Legacy, Heritage and the Untamed Charm of Nature

India Glycols is one of the finest spirits producers in India offering a perfect balance of craftsmanship and flavor. Made with the finest ingredients and expert distillation, it delivers a smooth, bold taste that captures the spirit of excellence in every bottle.

Unlocking the special mystery of our premium spirits, it's a journey from our multi-column distillation to hand-selected botanicals, creating an alcohol that captivates the senses.

With three generations of expertise, this process creates a smooth and refined texture making it the finest in India. Each sip uncovers a wild, untamed flavor, echoing the land's essence and hidden wonders within every drop.
